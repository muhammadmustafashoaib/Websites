Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We’ve answered some of the most common ones below. Still unsure? Feel free to get in touch.

🚰 What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve the entire metropolitan area and surrounding suburbs. If you’re unsure whether we can reach you, give us a call and we’ll let you know!

🕒 Do you offer 24/7 emergency services?

Absolutely — plumbing problems don’t follow a 9-to-5 schedule, and neither do we. Our team is available 24/7 for emergency repairs and urgent plumbing issues.

💧 How do I know if I have a hidden leak?

Signs include unusually high water bills, damp spots, mold growth, or the sound of running water when all taps are off. If you suspect a leak, contact us for a professional inspection.

🏡 Can you help with bathroom or kitchen remodeling?

Yes! We specialize in full-service plumbing for remodeling projects, including installing new fixtures, rerouting pipes, and ensuring everything is up to code.

💳 What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit/debit cards, and online transfers. Financing options are also available for larger projects — ask our team for details.
